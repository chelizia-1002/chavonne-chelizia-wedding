Children are warmly welcomed and can be added to your RSVP at the following rates (as per the venue):

Ages 0–5 years: R450 per child

Ages 6–12 years: R550 per child

Should you wish to bring a partner we have not extended an invite to, please contact us before submitting your RSVP.

Thank you for your understanding and support. We cannot wait to celebrate this unforgettable day with you.
